About us

About us...



Welcome to Blissful Moments – where each candle tells a story, and every scent brings you closer to serenity. We’re not just about wax and wicks — we’re about creating moments of peace, joy, and connection in a fast-moving world. Whether it’s the warm glow during your evening unwind, the gentle fragrance that calms a busy mind, or the thoughtful gift that makes someone’s day — our candles are made to spark something special.


Our Story


Blissful Moments was born out of a simple idea: to turn everyday routines into beautiful rituals. What started as a small passion project grew into a heartfelt mission — to handcraft candles that bring light, comfort, and mindfulness into your space.
Inspired by nature, emotions, and everyday beauty, we blend high-quality soy wax, clean fragrances, and elegant designs to craft pieces that are as aesthetic as they are aromatic.

My love for technology extends to programming, where I thrive on building creative and functional projects. Beyond coding, I’m a creative soul who finds inspiration in sketching. For me, sketching is more than just a hobby—it’s a way to express emotions and capture the beauty of the world around me. With every pencil stroke, I aim to bring ideas to life on paper, whether it’s a realistic portrait or an imaginative design.
